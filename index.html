Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app integrated with Instagram Graph API.

Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to interact with Instagram Messaging API (such as messages sent to the connected Instagram Business Account). No personal data is stored or shared with third parties.

How We Use Information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. Data is only transmitted securely between Instagram and our app as required by the API.
